Welcome to CJ Supplies

About Us

C J Supplies were established in 2002. We are a small family run business who know all our customers on a personal level and understand their requirements.

It's always easy to say how great you are but I believe consistently servicing retailers with thermal paper rolls and other Point of Sale consumables over a 15 year period speaks for itself.

We also supply a wide range of laser toner and ink jet cartridges for most well known brands and printer models.

We Know Our Customer

We know our customer, we really do!

It's the basis of our 15 year longevity supplying the retail industry with Point of Sale consumables.

We offer the benefits of dealing with a small family run business. Our customers are not just numbers on a spreadsheet but people who we develop individual relationships with, therefore offering the best possible service in an approachable and professional fashion.

Retailers

We know that different retailers have their own specific needs and requirements. We are happy to discuss the possibility of special terms on a customer to customer basis unlike some more rigid organisations.

Nobody's Perfect

If we ever do make a rare mistake we will rectify it in a speedy and satisfactory manner. ​ We wholeheartedly believe, in the sadly somewhat outdated adage, that the customer is always right!
